Mission Statement:

Our mission here at Cook's bakery is to make every pie,cupcake and bread with the care and quality that you would feel as if you had it made it your self. We are located in Morris, Minnesota just Northwest of the Twin-Cities area. We are a proud family locally owned bakery. We make our dough from scratch everyday with love.
